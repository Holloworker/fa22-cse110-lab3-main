The three Body Problem (Chinese: 三体; lit. 'Three-Body') is a science fiction novel written by the Chinese writer Liu Cixin. The title refers to the three-body problem in orbital mechanics. It is the first novel of the Remembrance of Earth's Past (Chinese: 地球往事) trilogy, but the whole series is normally referred to as The Three-Body Problem. [1] The trilogy's second and third novels are The Dark Forest and Death's End respectively
